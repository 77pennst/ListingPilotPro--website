What we look for

Most Amazon listings lose sales because the product is not explained clearly enough or PPC is spending before the listing is ready.

  • Weak title structure or missing main keywords.
  • Images that do not answer buyer questions fast enough.
  • PPC campaigns spending on low-intent searches.
  • Variation problems that split reviews or traffic.
  • Backend catalog issues blocking listing updates.
  • Missed competitor ASIN targeting opportunities.
